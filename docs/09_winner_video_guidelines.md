# Winner video guidelines

This video is **only required for teams that win**. It is separate from the **participant intro video** (30–60 seconds) that all teams must submit with their project.

If you win, we need a short pitch/demo video from you before we send the prize. We’ll cut these into one winners reel for the Conflux ecosystem.
**Video Requirements:**
- **Language**: English or your native language
- **Format**: 16:9 landscape
- **Framing**: Front facing, clean audio, not a messy background

**Video Structure:**
1. **Quick Intro** (30-60 seconds)
   - Hi, I'm [Name], from [Country]. I'm [Role]. I joined Conflux in [Year] and I'm one of the winners of Global Hackfest 2026.

2. **Project Basics** (1-2 minutes)
   - Project name and team
   - What you built
   - Why you built it, what inspired it
   - What the solution does

3. **Why Conflux Network?** (30-60 seconds)
   - Explain why you chose Conflux for your project

4. **Close** (10-20 seconds)
   - Thank you Conflux Network


**Practical tips:** Good light on your face (e.g. by a window). Use a tripod or something stable. Speak clearly.
