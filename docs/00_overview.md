# Global Hackfest 2026 — Overview

Four-week virtual hackathon on Conflux. No fixed tracks. You build what you want; we score repos, docs, and demo videos.

## How it works

1. Register on [Luma](https://luma.com/vniqj3eg). Join (March 23 – April 20, 2026). Solo or teams of up to 5.
2. Build for 4 weeks. Discord and office hours available.
3. Submit: add your project under `/projects/` with demo video, required docs, and a **participant intro video** (30–60 seconds, for use at a conference in Hong Kong). Check the submission checklist and submission guide.
4. We pick 10 award recipients (5 Main Awards, 5 Category Awards). They send short pitch videos for a single winners showcase video. No live presentations.

## Dates

- Submissions due: April 20, 2026, 11:59:59
- Judging: 1 week (starting April 21, 2026), no live rounds
- Winners: April 27, 2026

## Scoring

We score: technical quality (25%), Conflux integration (25%), UX (20%), innovation (20%), presentation and docs (10%).

## Before you start

Join Telegram and Discord. Skim the rules and the resources doc so you know what you need to submit. Form a team if you want. Pick a direction (or don’t). Set up your dev environment and a Conflux wallet. Kickoff and workshops are on the calendar; office hours are there when you’re stuck.
